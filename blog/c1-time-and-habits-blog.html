Cultural - Time and habits

29 October 2017

Which timebox and habits techniques did you try?

I tried to do meditation before sleep and doing task at smaller steps for bigger tasks. I found that I need to take a short break after 25 mins. The small break seems quite similar to Pomodoro Technique, but not exactly. For Pomodoro-technique, it also involve review what has been done, and the break is only at about 5 mins, not more.

What did you find interesting?

The Fogg Method is really interesting. Its technique of "trigger, tiny habit, then celebrate" is really reliable for anyone to change own's behaviour easily. I like his example of "After I pee, I will do two push up. Then celebrate by saying I'm awesome!"

What worked for you?

Breaking down a task to a smaller tasks has worked for me. Following my natural rhythms also worked for me.

Which will you eperiment further with?

I will experiment further with Pomodoro Technique and Fogg Method. One is about accurate time estimation on tiny task; another is to change habit reliably. When both of them are combined together, they are really powerful.
