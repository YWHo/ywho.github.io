How did the 'process over product' concept affect the way you tackled the site redesign and rebuild?

The 'process over product' concept keep me in check. I was no longer feeling the rush to deliver the product. I was no longer feeling the pressue to aim for perfection. I didn't spent too much in planning until I paralyzed. By breaking up a big task into several smaller tasks, the execution seems easy. For example, while doing wireframe, I focus on the overall look and feel, not bothering on other details such as color and font. While implementing webpage on wireframe, my focus is about how to implement, not about changing design at the same time. After the wireframe part is implemented, it was like 50% of product has been done. The rest of details such as font, color, links, content etc, all felt easy to do.
